Sessions

Powerful divinations reveal that the eldritch beings beyond time and space interact with the five through unimaginable interfaces known as “sessions.” The eldritch being that some call “Ross” and others call the “Dungeon Master” names these sessions. I have transcribed them, and for some of them I have scribbled a brief summary.

Arc Two: In Progress

021 The Gorgon Slain The party escapes from the crumbling tower. Anemone warns Leonus about the dangers of his new mark. The party learns that the BBEG killed Saron, seizes his ship, and accidentally rams it into their old folding boat. Degaan is almost killed. Mrs. Fortune orders Valon to bring Leonus to the Daughters of Sora Kell.
022 Captain Leonus and the Starbreeze Crew The party figures out how to control the Cannith ship and what Saron’s magic items do. Anemone drags the Cannith and warforged corpses aboard. The party arrives back at Glumtown.
023 Duty Calls The party takes in the bloodbath that is Glumtown, persuades the lampmaker to leave with them, and arrives back in Zarash’ak. Different sects of House Tharashk fight amongst each other as Degaan and Valon save Anemone from being cut in half. The unmarked civilians flee with the party as the House sacrifices itself to the BBEG.
024 Fallout The party talks to the refugees, learns from an elder about the existence of nearby Urthhold, and brings the refugees there. Valon launches himself around the various refugee ships using his grappling hook. Leonus reveals the Mark of Death to a ship full of refugees and frightens everyone around him. Saron d’Cannith’s body is preserved using oil. The party has several difficult conversations.
025 Endings & Beginnings The party returns to Zarash’ak to investigate. They bury the dead while Anemone supervises. The party journeys back to Vralkek to chat, shop, and definitely not make deals with hags.
026 The Best Laid Plans The party finishes shopping at Vralkek, makes plans to travel up to the Great Crag, and relaxes before their big journey. Baabi becomes an apprentice to Mrs. Fortune. Anemone makes a discovery about the nature of the dragonmark-draining magic of the BBEG.
027 The Hidden Demesne Pepper becomes De’Vaa, a muscular half-orc. She contacts the mysterious woman on Saron's sending stone. The party heads towards the ruins, yaps, camps, yaps some more, and gets caught in a memory threshold trap before they figure out that yeeting each other is the correct way through.
028 New “Friends” The party fends off were-people and meet Bazgul. Together, they discover that the demesne is a secret info trading tower filled with faceless shadow people.
029 Information Exchange Everyone introduces themselves and exchanges info with the brokers. Lycanthropy is removed from two party members. Pepper learns that her sister, Sycamore, is still alive. Valon has a great time jumping. Anemone learns that House Therani wants everyone in the party dead.
030 Popcorn and Pranks The party arrives back to the ship, finds dead werewolves killed by the defense system, and has a chat about their potential enemies. De’Vaa speaks to the sister of Saron dâ€™Cannith. The party pranks Anemone with spells and makeup. Anemone thinks she’s in a nightmare, which forces repressed memories of red lightning and grey stone to the surface. The party arrives in Znir.
031 Gnollst Stories The party arrives in Znir, speaks with the locals, and prepares to leave with a caravan in two days. Much nonsense ensues.
032 The Dark Alpha The party travels for a week and two days before a warlord of Droaam defeats everyone and bring them before the Daughters of Sora Kell.
033 The Daughters of Sora Kell The party meets two of the three Daughters and become honored guests of Droaam. During dinner, they learn that there are 13 daughters instead of 3, that Sora Kell is dead, and that Breland has good defenses against divination. Valon fights in the Pits. Anemone and Pepper meet Sora Teraza. Leonus suffers from dehydration and dry skin and gets his vials of diseased blood back.
034 Visions and Dreams The party meets Segue Montague, who joins the party. Leonus learns to be less creepy and makes a dryad friend. Pepper learns that Sycamore is at the Sharn refugee camp in the Middle Central Plateau. Sora Katra agrees to continue searching for more info about Sycamore. Anemone gets an expensive dragonshard necklace and finishes figuring out the portal to Xoriat, the Realm of Madness. Valon dedicates his life to becoming a protector and manifests a Mark of Sentinel. Sora Maenya gifts Valon bracers of ogre strength.
035 Driving a Shard Bargain Leonus performs a favor for the Daughters by attempting to call Sora Kell’s spirit from Dolurrh. He fails to find it, but is nonetheless awarded with dragonshards and a Vol artifact. The party all interact for the first time.
036 These Weapons are Fire The party shops around for silver weapons and meets Vuul’kaan, the fire giant craftsman. Valon brings his dinosaur back. Anemone tells the party about the red lightning and rip in reality that will occur in Sharn. The boat's engine is fixed up and the party prepares to leave.
037 Confessions Separately, the party has several vulnerable conversations. No one enjoys Anemone’s seawater popsicles and rat pancakes. Segue reveals that he is actually named Felix. Pepper and Felix teach Valon how to read. Leonus is emotionally disturbed and is given some assistance by Vash. Pepper and Anemone question the corpse of Saron d’Cannith. Anemone calculates that they can use dragonshards to power home to Sharn.
038 Quiet Night In Anemone stays on the boat optimizing the engine. The rest of the party hang out in Vralkek. Valon picks up a magic backpack, learns that his mom is alive, and gets his double-bladed scimitar Perf’d. Leonus says hi to Baabi and gets a staff of healing. Pepper has a talk with Vash about loss and family. The party spends a quiet night playing cards on the ship. Pepper is offered captain status but turns the position down.
039 Make More Good When There is More Bad The party has several interactions in the first week of their trip back to Sharn. Pepper becomes captain of the Starbreeze and learns about the silverborough trees. Leonus's actions cause Felix to be concerned for his neurological health. Felix and Vash bond. Valon teaches Hawthorn how to transform and fly. Anemone has Pepper send off a message to Blades and laughs like a supervillain. The party talks about roles.
